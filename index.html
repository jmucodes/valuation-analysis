Market Analysis

Dominican Republic iGaming — Ground Floor of a Regulated Market

The DR formally regulated online gambling in 2024 under Resolution 136-2024. Sports betting licenses cost $260K, online casino licenses $346K, and they're valid for 5 years. The licensing process averages ~2 years, creating a significant barrier to entry and first-mover advantage for holders.

Key Insight: With only 4 active licensees and a 2-year application process, Appuesta's near-final license status is itself a scarce, defensible asset. A new competitor entering today would not be operational until 2028.

TAM Calculation

With 4 licensed operators (soon to be 5 with Appuesta), even conservative market share assumptions yield significant revenue potential. Capturing just 3-5% of the TAM in year 1-2 would represent $10M-$17M in annual revenue.

LATAM iGaming Macro Tailwinds

  • The LATAM iGaming market reached $6B in 2025, with a projected CAGR of 18.4% through 2028 toward ~$10B
  • The DR's regulatory overhaul (Resolution 136-2024 + proposed DGJA regulator in June 2025) signals government commitment to a transparent, growing market
  • Google's $500M IXP investment in DR improves local infrastructure, reducing latency and hosting costs for digital platforms
  • 66% of DR e-commerce payments are via credit card — Appuesta's secured local bank accounts and CARDNET integration position it perfectly
  • DR smartphone penetration approaching 90% by 2029 projections

Deal Structure

Recommended Instrument & Terms

Option A: SAFE Note

Simple Agreement for Future Equity — standard for pre-seed/seed. No interest, no maturity date. Converts at next priced round.

  • Valuation cap: $4M-$5M
  • No discount (or 10% max)
  • Pro-rata rights for investor
  • Most founder-friendly option
RECOMMENDED
Option B: Priced Equity Round

Direct equity sale. More complex but gives investor immediate ownership stake and clear terms.

  • Pre-money: $4M
  • Board observer seat (no vote)
  • Quarterly reporting requirement
  • Preferred shares with 1x liquidation preference
ALTERNATIVE

For a raise of this size ($500K-$750K) from likely angel investors or strategic partners in DR, a SAFE note is simpler and faster. If raising from an institutional VC or a single large investor, a priced round may be preferred.

Key Talking Points

  • Scarcity: The DR license is a finite, time-limited asset. Only 4 other operators exist. The 2-year application process is the moat — not technology.
  • Timing: The market is in its infancy. Regulation passed in 2024. This is the equivalent of entering US sports betting in 2019 — before the market exploded.
  • Capital Efficiency: $438K already deployed. Product is built. This isn't a "build" raise, it's a "launch" raise. Every dollar goes to customer acquisition and market penetration.
  • Network Effect: Escogido partnership + founder connections create marketing advantages that competitors (especially foreign operators) simply cannot replicate with money alone.
  • Payment Infrastructure: Local bank accounts in DR for an iGaming company is exceptionally rare and valuable. Most offshore operators rely on e-wallets and crypto — Appuesta can accept bank transfers and credit cards directly.
  • Exit Paths: Acquisition by larger LATAM operator (Betsson, Codere, Rush Street), or scale to Series A once DR revenue data proves the model.
